STRATEGIC PROFIT MODEL

The strategic profit model enables managers to estimate the impact of supply chain improvements such as cycle time reduction on balance sheets and income statement accounts (i.e., sales and inventory accounts). The ultimate financial aim of SCM is to increase return on equity (ROE). The strategic profit model can be used to trace the impact of SCM initiatives on return on assets (ROA), profit margin, ROE, and other financial metrics.
